Welcome to the ACCT# extractor tool!

To use effectively, hit Ctrl+A to select the entire text content of any reminder you are working, then Ctrl+C to copy the selected text. Then use Ctrl+V to paste the copied text into the input area, and the account numbers will be extracted into the output area. You can then click the button below the output area to copy the numbers directly to your keyboard, and then paste them directly into the Epic account record.

⬆️ You can paste any copied text above ⬆️
This tool processes all data locally and does not store or transmit any information.
